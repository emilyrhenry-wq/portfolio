The Work

A resource hub for a WHO Europe and UNICEF-partnered initiative

Led the end-to-end development of the Health Systems for Early Childhood Development website — a digital resource hub created in partnership with WHO Europe and UNICEF. The platform supports professionals across regions with evidence-based tools and guidance to strengthen family-centered health systems for young children.

My Contribution

  • Coordinated the full project lifecycle, from initial scoping and brand alignment to final delivery
  • Served as the key liaison between content, design, and technical teams to ensure a cohesive, user-centered build
  • Ensured the website reflected the initiative's cross-sectoral mission by integrating insights from health systems experts, early childhood practitioners, and policymakers
  • Oversaw the creation of a platform that supports caregivers and practitioners with accessible, research-driven resources
