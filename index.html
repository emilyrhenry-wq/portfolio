About

Comms that serve the work.

I've spent my career at the crossroads of communications, donor relations, and education — working with organizations to turn ambitious initiatives into concrete, human-centered materials that resonate across cultures and contexts.

Over the last 10 years, I've coordinated communications for global events and digital campaigns, managed multi-country publications, ran webinar series with WHO and UNICEF, and helped rebuild digital platforms that serve networks of educators, policymakers, and families worldwide.

My academic background in international peace studies and study of post-genocide reconciliation in Rwanda shaped an approach to communication that is always attuned to context and connects people. Stories that allow the people closest to the work to shine through. Throughout my career, I have used this lesson to help NGOs and non-profits showcase their unique impact.

I'm currently available for freelance, contract, and full-time roles in international communications, campaign management, donor engagement, and publication development.
