The Work

Strategic communications for a WHO and UNICEF–partnered series

Strong communications are essential to the success of any webinar series. For this WHO Europe and UNICEF ECARO–partnered initiative, I led the strategic communications efforts across the full lifecycle of the series — from pre-event outreach and stakeholder coordination to post-session dissemination and follow-up. The series ultimately reached more than 4,000 participants.

My Contribution

I managed communications tasks from start to finish, ensuring each session reached the right audiences and that partner messaging remained aligned throughout. After each event, I coordinated the distribution of recordings, resources, and summaries to extend impact well beyond the live sessions.

Impact

The series brought together policymakers, health professionals, and early childhood practitioners across Europe and Central Asia around a shared goal: strengthening health systems to deliver family-centered care for young children. It sparked interest from other organizations to work on the same aims.
